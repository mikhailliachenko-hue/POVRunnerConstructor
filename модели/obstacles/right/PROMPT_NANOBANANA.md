# Nano Banana — препятствие RIGHT

Модель должна визуально перекрывать левую и центральную часть дороги, оставляя очевидный проход справа.

```text
Create a single production-quality 3D game-asset concept image of a RIGHT-DODGE GATE for a first-person endless runner in a bright stylized voxel/block world. Design one connected freestanding obstacle structure that blocks the LEFT and CENTER portions of a three-lane road while leaving a large, unmistakable rectangular passage on the RIGHT. The right opening is part of the geometry and must remain completely empty from ground to above player height; the solid mass must be visibly concentrated on the left and center. Build the structure as a chunky modular barricade: thick block-stone columns, reinforced dark-metal braces, a broad solid wall section over the left and center lanes, and a short stable base only beneath the blocked area. Approximate overall proportions: 7 m wide, 4.8 m tall, 1.6 m deep; right passage about one third of total width and at least 2.8 m high. The silhouette itself must communicate “move right” without using arrows, writing, signs, icons, or painted instructions.

Use original Minecraft-inspired styling without copying any exact existing game asset: beveled voxel blocks, readable square construction, clean low-to-medium complexity, hand-painted pixel-like PBR textures, warm sandstone and muted rust-red blocks, charcoal metal reinforcement, restrained golden accent blocks only on the safe right edge. Every visible component must be thick, physically supported, connected, and suitable for a watertight 3D mesh. No thin poles, wires, chains, cloth, glass, transparent surfaces, floating pieces, rubble particles, characters, creatures, or moving parts.

Show exactly ONE isolated gate, completely visible and centered, in a nearly orthographic three-quarter FRONT view from slightly above. The front face and the right-side opening must be immediately readable; do not mirror the design and do not put the opening on the left. Use a 70 mm product-visualization lens with minimal distortion. Place it directly on an invisible flat ground plane against a neutral light-gray seamless studio background. Soft large-area key light from upper left, balanced fill, subtle contact shadow, even exposure, sharp focus, high texture clarity. Keep clear empty margin around the complete object.

Only the single obstacle and its soft contact shadow may appear. No runner, no road, no environment, no extra barrier, no pedestal, no base platform beyond the object’s structural feet, no text, no arrow, no logo, no watermark, no UI, no border, no cropped parts, no duplicate. Render as a believable textured 3D asset reference suitable for conversion to GLB. Square 1:1 image, high resolution, object occupying roughly 80% of the canvas.
```

🎯 Target: Nano Banana, 💡 оптимизировано для однозначного прохода справа без риска зеркальной трактовки.
