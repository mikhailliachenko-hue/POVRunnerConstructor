# Nano Banana — препятствие SMASH

Модель должна выглядеть как хрупкая стена, которую игроку понятно нужно пробить.

```text
Create a single production-quality 3D game-asset concept image of a BREAKABLE SMASH WALL for a first-person endless runner in a bright stylized voxel/block world. Design one broad solid wall spanning a three-lane road, approximately 10.5 m wide, 4.6 m tall, and 0.55 m thick. It must instantly read as an obstacle intended to be smashed through rather than jumped over or avoided. Construct it from large lightweight-looking voxel masonry blocks held in a simple dark-metal outer frame. Add a clear pre-weakened central impact zone using deep branching cracks and several displaced-but-still-attached block segments, forming a readable circular or roughly player-width fracture area in the center. The wall must remain a single connected intact object before impact: no open hole, no detached rubble, no flying debris. Make the central fracture visibly weaker than the outer frame while preserving enough thickness for image-to-3D reconstruction.

Use an original Minecraft-inspired visual language without copying an exact game asset: chunky beveled blocks, warm coral-red and burnt-orange masonry, dark graphite frame, glowing-looking yellow-orange material visible only deep inside the cracks, hand-painted pixel-like PBR textures, subtle roughness variation, chipped edges and impact stress. Keep the construction low-to-medium complexity and physically coherent. Avoid thin shards, tiny fragments, wires, chains, text, arrows, target symbols, characters, transparent surfaces, smoke, fire, particles, or floating pieces. The result must be suitable for a static “intact but cracked” GLB model; do not depict an explosion or animation.

Show exactly ONE isolated wall, completely visible and centered, from a nearly straight-on three-quarter FRONT view with only a slight view of one side and the top thickness. Use a 70–85 mm lens with minimal distortion so the front cracking pattern is dominant and symmetrical enough for reconstruction. Place it directly on an invisible flat ground plane against a neutral light-gray seamless studio background. Soft large-area key light from upper left, balanced frontal fill, subtle contact shadow beneath the full wall, even exposure, sharp focus, high texture clarity, no dramatic environment lighting. Leave clear empty margin around every edge.

Only the single wall and its soft contact shadow may appear. No road, no environment, no runner, no hammer or weapon, no platform, no pedestal, no extra wall, no words, no logo, no watermark, no UI, no border, no cropped geometry, no duplicate, no detached debris. Render as a believable textured 3D game asset reference suitable for image-to-3D conversion and GLB export. Square 1:1 image, high resolution, wall occupying about 80% of the canvas.
```

🎯 Target: Nano Banana, 💡 оптимизировано для цельной, но визуально разрушаемой стены SMASH без лишних частиц.
