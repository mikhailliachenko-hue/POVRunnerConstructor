# Nano Banana — препятствие DUCK

Модель должна создавать низкий поперечный просвет, под которым игроку очевидно нужно пригнуться.

```text
Create a single production-quality 3D game-asset concept image of a DUCK-UNDER GATE for a first-person endless runner in a bright stylized voxel/block world. Design one connected, stable overhead obstacle spanning the full width of a three-lane road. It must clearly require the player to crouch: two thick side supports hold a massive low horizontal beam, leaving one continuous wide opening underneath. The bottom of the beam should sit at approximately 1.65 m above ground, low enough to block a standing runner but high enough for a crouching runner. Approximate total proportions: 10.5 m wide, 4.7 m tall including supports, and 1.8 m deep. Make the overhead beam visually dominant, thick, heavy, and unmistakably solid. Keep the entire ground-level passage beneath it open across all lanes, apart from supports at the extreme far left and far right edges.

Use original Minecraft-inspired styling without copying any exact existing game asset: chunky beveled voxel geometry, dark steel frame, purple-blue reinforced blocks, bright magenta hazard bands embedded as material accents without text, subtle bolts represented as broad low-relief shapes, hand-painted pixel-like PBR textures, moderate scratches and edge wear. The design must be one coherent construction with believable load-bearing supports and thick connections. No hanging chains, ropes, cables, cloth, spikes, transparent pieces, floating objects, dangling signs, characters, or loose debris that could confuse image-to-3D reconstruction.

Show exactly ONE isolated gate fully visible in a nearly orthographic three-quarter FRONT view from slightly above, using a 70 mm lens with minimal perspective distortion. The open space beneath the beam must be clearly visible and unobstructed. Center the gate on a neutral light-gray seamless studio background, standing directly on an invisible flat ground plane. Use soft large-area lighting from upper left, gentle fill, subtle contact shadows under both supports, even exposure, sharp focus, and clear material separation. Preserve empty margin on all sides and above the object.

The image must include only the single gate and its soft contact shadows. No runner, no road, no scenery, no additional gate, no platform, no pedestal, no words, no letters, no arrows, no logo, no watermark, no UI, no border, no cropping, no duplicate. Render it as a believable textured 3D game asset suitable as an image-to-3D reference and later GLB export. Square 1:1 composition, high resolution, object occupying approximately 80% of the canvas.
```

🎯 Target: Nano Banana, 💡 оптимизировано для хорошо читаемого низкого просвета и цельной конструкции DUCK.
